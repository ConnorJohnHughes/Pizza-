Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is the recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

    2 eggs
      1 lbs butter
      2 lbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter
  2. Mix the eggs
  3. Mix butter with eggs and cream
  4. Use spatula and fold the eggs and flip every 20 seconds in the pan
  5. When eggs are still moist remove fro heat
